available** (target window ~2026-05-26). The audit explicitly recommends
running R1 (telemetry) for 30 days before R5 (decide §7) so the cuts are
data-driven, not assumption-driven. Do not revisit this decision earlier
unless usage telemetry shows something extreme (e.g. zero hits on five
endpoints).
